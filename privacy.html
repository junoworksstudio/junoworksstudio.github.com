Junoworks Privacy Policy

Junoworks is a gaming studio that creates and publishes free mobile games for entertainment.

The purpose of this policy is to provide you with all the important information about how and why your data may be collected and processed by Junoworks when you use one of our applications. This privacy policy governs and details the principles Junoworks applies to the data we collect and process to:

  1. Develop and publish mobile games
  2. Serve advertising that are tailored to our users
  3. Run our website (Junoworks.co)

This policy also aims to remind you about your rights and provide you with the resources if you need to exercise them. If you have any questions related to this policy or our practices, don’t hesitate to contact us at our contact information listed below.

Why does Junoworks collect data from its games?

When you install and use a Junoworks app, we collect and process data for different legitimate purposes.

Junoworks collects data to:

  1. Run analytics and understand how users interact with our product and services in order to improve it
  2. Junoworks only processes or shares personal data collected through Junoworks apps for analytics purposes once you express your consent through the popup notice included in our apps.

Who your data might be shared with & why

Junoworks does not share your personal data with third parties with your prior consent. When you consent to the collection of data for advertising and analytics purposes, we may share the data listed above with the following recipients:

  1. Ad Partners that allow us to monetize the ad inventory of our apps and provide users with free products and services
  2. These partners usually collect data via their own tools (SDKs). You will find a list of our partners implementing advertising SDKs through our applications and the privacy policies of their services that describe their practices and allow you to exercise your rights directly toward them hereafter:
  3. Applovin - https://www.applovin.com/privacy/
  4. Mopub - https://www.mopub.com/legal/privacy/
  5. Analytics companies that provide us with tools and technologies that allow us to better understand our users and how they interact with our services. Those partners also usually collect data via their own SDKs. You will find a list of our partners implementing analytical SDKs through our applications hereafter:
  6. Kochava - https://www.kochava.com/support-privacy/
  7. Amplitude - https://amplitude.com/privacy
  8. Authorities - only to the extent we are obliged to by laws and regulations

Why does Junoworks collect data for advertising purposes?

Junoworks ads services allow Junoworks and its publisher partners (most of the time Junoworks ads partners are third party mobile application providers) to monetize advertising inventory by allowing advertisers to serve personalized advertising to users.

Below are reasons why Junoworks may collect data:

  1. To help advertiser partners monetizer their ad inventory - Junoworks helps advertisers promote their products and services to an audience that has demonstrated interest in similar products and services and allows them to optimize their marketing spend.
  2. Improve Junoworks Ads - Junoworks constantly improves its services and to do so, Junoworks processes the data listed below to allow us to better understand how users interact with ads to optimize the user experience.

We contractually oblige our partners to collect the express consent of their users before any data is sent to Junoworks.

What data does Junoworks collect for advertising?

For the purposes listed above, the data Junoworks will collect is limited to:

  1. The apps you are using
  2. IP address
  3. Mobile Advertising ID (Apple IDFA or Google AAID)
  4. Technical information about the device you user and your connection
  5. Data pertaining to your interactions with the advertisements served

With who is your data shared?

Data is collected for ad services are only shared with the following categories of recipients and only to the extent that’s necessary for their purpose:

  1. Advertisers that are using Junoworks Ads to serve personalized or non-personalized advertising

How long does Junoworks keep your data?

Personal data collected for the purposes described are not kept longer than necessary for the purposes described above. Junoworks does not retain such data for more than 13 months.

Children Data

These Services do not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. In the case we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we immediately delete this from our servers. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so that we will be able to do necessary actions.

Changing or deleting your information

You may contact us regarding any questions or comments regarding this Privacy Policy or with any request to change or delete your personal information by emailing us at junoworksstudio@gmail.com.

You can also reset your mobile advertising identifier (IDFA on iOS, GAAID on Android) or activate “Limit Ad Tracking” in your device settings in order to make previously collected data non linkable to you or your device anymore and limit further data collection.

California Privacy Rights

If you are a California resident and the processing of your information is subject to the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), you have certain rights with respect to that inforamation.

Right to know and right to delete - You have a right to request what information we collect, use, disclose, and sell. Much of this information that’s used is already included in this policy. You may make a “request to know” or “request to delete” your personal information (subject to verification of your identity) by contacting us at junoworksstudio@gmail.com

Right to opt out - You have the right to opt out of future “sales” of personal information.

Changes to this policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. These changes are effective immediately after they are posted on this page.

Contact
